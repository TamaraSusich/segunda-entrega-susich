What We Do

We design and execute strategies for the development of innovative products and objects with strong market potential. We are a team passionate about the creative process and the different stages an object goes through until it is finally born. We believe in collaborative work between our clients, their brands, ourselves, and other professionals related to the product that is being developed. We are passionate about simple, functional objects with identity. Objects that have value and that are capable of transmitting a message and remain in the memory of those who consume it. At SICH, we manage to understand and address the opportunities that this globalized world offers us, and we take advantage of them to learn about other ways of creating.

Tatiana Susich-Founder of SICH

"SICH is an expression of connectivity DESIGN - INDUSTRY - CULTURE. Being part of the process in which an abstract idea advances, takes shape and materializes into a functional object with strong identity, is a magical experience - that we enjoy every day!"
