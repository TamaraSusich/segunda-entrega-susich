Herb Grinder

  • Client: Aequi
  • Location: Bulgaria
  • Year: 2020-2021
  • Development: Concept, creation, product design development, design for manufacturing, prototype management.

Cannabis use is growing culture. More and more countries legalize its medicinal and recreational consumption, and the general public accepts it. Aequi's mission is to create and provide quality cannabis accesories that enhance the smoking experience and identity. With SICH, we create a unique grinder that acts as a small decoration object, proud of its function.

Manufactured under CNC process in aluminium, it will come in a small range of colors. we are currentlyunder the final prototyping process.

Cereamic Family For Fermented Food

  • Client: Symbiocity
  • Location: Sydney, Australia
  • Year: 2019
  • Development: Concept, creation, product design development, design for manufacturing, prototype management.

Symbiocity is a family of ceramic products destined to elaborate fermented food in the home. Due to their hight probiotic content, fermented foods can give you inmune system boost and reduce your risk of infections.A kambucha jar, crock, and kefir marker integrate the family. Each product has a preservation system thats respects ancient techniques but also includes modern sealing systems.

Jewelry Family

  • Client: Woodwatch
  • Location: Netherlands
  • Year: 2021
  • Development: Concept, creation, product design development, design for manufacturing, prototype management.

The inspiration fot WW Jewelry comes directly from its pillars as a company and the characteristics of its products. Brinding natural materials, such as wood, metal, and marble, to objects of daily use, and revaluing them in "luxury" products, was the development scope. Those are authentic pieces with craftsmanship developments. Each piece's delicacy and small details are a fundamental part of the inspiration. Deciding to move foward with elements of pure geometry speaks of the purity of the pieces, the components, and each chosen material.
